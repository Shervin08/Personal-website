Education

Primary School

I am a student at Jerek National School. I have attended this institution from the age of seven to twelve. I am the auxiliary superintendent of the institution. UPSR results obtained include four A's and one B.I am accountable for the supervision of a variety of administrative responsibilities and the collaboration with instructors to guarantee a learning environment that is conducive to all students in my capacity as an associate superintendent.

Secondary School

Subsequently, I transferred to Tengku Bendahara National Secondary School in Gua Musang, where I achieved 5A, 2B, and 1C at the SPM level.In addition to environmental conservation programs and debate society, I was an active participant in a variety of extracurricular activities.
